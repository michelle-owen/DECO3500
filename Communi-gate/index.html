WELCOME!

This website has been created to help STEM students develop communication skills through team activities. You will have the opportunity to approach multiple problems as a team, with the aim of varying roles within the team and practising team communication.

Before you get started, get each member of your team of 4 to pick a DIFFERENT number. After all selecting a DIFFERENT number, select a player view using your number from the main menu of the top of the screen.
